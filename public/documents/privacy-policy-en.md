## 8. Your Rights Under GDPR

As a data subject, you have the following rights:

### 8.1 Right of Access (Art. 15)
- Request confirmation of whether we process your data
- Obtain a copy of your personal data
- Request information about processing purposes

**How to exercise:** Email privacy@wohnnomade.com with subject "Access Request"

### 8.2 Right to Rectification (Art. 16)
- Correct inaccurate personal data
- Complete incomplete personal data

**How to exercise:** Log into your account or email privacy@wohnnomade.com

### 8.3 Right to Erasure / "Right to be Forgotten" (Art. 17)
You may request deletion when:
- Data is no longer necessary for original purpose
- You withdraw consent (when consent was the legal basis)
- You object to processing (and no overriding legitimate grounds exist)
- Data was unlawfully processed
- Legal obligation requires deletion

**Important Limitations:**
- We may refuse deletion if we have overriding legitimate interests
- Entries based on valid contractual consent may be retained
- Legal defense purposes may require retention

**How to exercise:** Email privacy@wohnnomade.com with subject "Deletion Request"

### 8.4 Right to Restriction of Processing (Art. 18)
Request temporary restriction when:
- Accuracy of data is contested
- Processing is unlawful but you prefer restriction over deletion
- We no longer need the data, but you need it for legal claims

**How to exercise:** Email privacy@wohnnomade.com with subject "Restriction Request"

### 8.5 Right to Data Portability (Art. 20)
- Receive your data in structured, common format
- Transmit data to another controller

**How to exercise:** Email privacy@wohnnomade.com with subject "Data Portability"

### 8.6 Right to Object (Art. 21)
- Object to processing based on legitimate interests
- Object to direct marketing at any time

**How to exercise:** Email privacy@wohnnomade.com with subject "Objection"

### 8.7 Automated Decision-Making (Art. 22)
We do not use automated decision-making or profiling that produces legal effects.

### 8.8 Right to Withdraw Consent
If processing is based on consent, you may withdraw consent at any time (this does not affect the lawfulness of processing before withdrawal).

### 8.9 Response Timeline
We will respond to all requests within 30 days. In complex cases, we may extend this by another 60 days with justification.
